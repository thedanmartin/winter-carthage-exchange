Art 377: Interactive Design 1

Web Type Specimen

This project asked students to investigate a web font that they were not previously familiar with and create a type specimen* website for that font. Students were required to include several different presentations of their chosen typeface including display, body copy, and experimental usage, along with a brief description of the typeface including information about its designer.

*In typography, type specimen refers to a presentation of a certain typeface to showcase its design and/or use.

Art 387: Special Topics in Graphic Design

Mobile-Friendly Web Collection

This project involved the students interpreting a collection for the web in a presentation that includes layouts appropriate for both mobile and computer users. Conceptually, the form of the presentation is influenced by the items and structure of the collection.

Art 477: Interactive Design 2

The Ambassador Website Redesigned

This project challenged students to re-imagine the website of a popular Upper Michigan restaurant. The pandemic has meant a shift in the restaurant’s business model as more people order takeout food and more tourists flock to the area looking for outdoor recreation opportunities. The existing website is cluttered and confusing—how might it be improved for a better user experience including a mobile-friendly interface, simplified navigation, and an attractive layout?

  • Hannah Hopkins
  • Jeremiah Wallace
  • Sarah Ison
